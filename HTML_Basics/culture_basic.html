Rwandan Culture

Rwanda has a rich and diverse culture, including traditional music, art, crafts, and customs that unite communities.

Rwandan Art and Crafts

Rwandan art includes Imigongo paintings, basket weaving, and traditional crafts.

Watch a video about Rwandan Imigongo art: Rwandan Imigongo Art Video

Rwandan Music Instruments

Traditional instruments like the Inanga and Umuduri are central to Rwandan music culture.

Popular Rwandan Crafts

  • Imigongo Paintings
  • Basket Weaving
  • Pottery

Traditional Music Instruments

  • Drums
    • Ingoma (war drum)
    • Umuduri (bamboo drum)
  • String Instruments
    • Inanga (zither)
    • Umwirongi (bow instrument)
  • Other Instruments
    • Ikembe (thumb piano)
