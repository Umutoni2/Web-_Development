Rwandan Culture

Rwanda has a rich and diverse culture, including traditional music, dance, art, and customs that unite communities.

Traditional Dances

Traditional dances are an important part of Rwandan heritage, often performed during ceremonies and celebrations.

  • Intore Dance
  • Umushayayo
  • Ibitekerezo

Traditional Foods

Rwandan cuisine reflects local ingredients and cultural practices.

  1. Isombe (cassava leaves with peanut sauce)
  2. Ugali (cassava or maize porridge)
  3. Brochettes (grilled meat skewers)

Traditional Instruments

Music is an important part of Rwandan culture. Traditional instruments include drums and string instruments.

  • Drums
    • Ingoma (war drum)
    • Umuduri (bamboo drum)
  • String Instruments
    • Inanga (traditional zither)
    • Umwirongi (bow instrument)
  • Other Instruments
    • Ikembe (thumb piano)
